About Us

Quvera Cloud was founded in 2018 by a team of passionate gamers and server administrators who were frustrated with the limitations of existing hosting solutions. We set out to create a hosting platform that combines enterprise-grade hardware with gaming-optimized software.

Today, we serve over 10,000 gaming communities worldwide, from small private servers to massive multiplayer networks. Our team of experts is available 24/7 to ensure your gaming experience is seamless and lag-free.
